Sunburst Chart

From 2014 to 2020, employment across states remained relatively stable. However, states like California (tech, entertainment, tourism, and national parks), Texas (tech, trade ports, military), New York (imports, exports, tech, tourism), and Florida (military and tourism) stand out due to their significant economic activities relative to their populations.
